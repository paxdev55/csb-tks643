How Our Content Gets To YOU

Want to know how our content gets to you, aka the order of operations where we send everything

1

Creation

First it's made, and then it comes on to the next step,
2

Publish to our Beta Site [Code Sandbox]

If it is an announcement, skip this. We post everything to our Code Sandbox to test it out (HTML, CSS, JS, and more) and then if we deem it good enough, it will go on to the next step,
3

Publish to this site

If it is for our club, skip this step. We're working on an announcements page, and it goes to beta, then it will head here! So, if it works out and we want to use it somewhere else we will...
4

Publish it to another place (optional) | Send the email

We will publish it to another site if we want to, aka our club. We will send the email to subscribers.
